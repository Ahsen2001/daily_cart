# DailyCart Google Play Store Publishing Guide

## Store Listing

App name: DailyCart  
Category: Shopping  
Content rating: Everyone  
Privacy policy: `https://dailycart.lk/privacy-policy`

## Short Description

DailyCart helps you order groceries, vegetables, fruits, household products, bakery items, pharmacy essentials, and daily necessities with fast delivery.

## Full Description

DailyCart is a modern grocery and daily essentials delivery app built for fast, simple, and reliable shopping. Customers can browse groceries, vegetables, fruits, household items, bakery products, beverages, pharmacy essentials, baby care products, personal care items, and more.

DailyCart supports easy search, wishlist, cart management, coupons, loyalty points, secure checkout, delivery scheduling, order tracking, customer reviews, and support tickets. Vendors can manage products and orders, while riders can view assigned deliveries, update delivery status, upload delivery proof, and manage their delivery workflow.

DailyCart is designed for Sri Lanka with LKR pricing, English language support, PayHere payments, Firebase notifications, and Google Maps delivery features.

## Required Assets

1. App icon: 512 x 512 PNG.
2. Feature graphic: 1024 x 500 PNG.
3. Phone screenshots:
   - Customer Home
   - Product Details
   - Cart
   - Checkout
   - Order Tracking
   - Vendor Dashboard
   - Vendor Products
   - Vendor Orders
   - Rider Deliveries
   - Rider Earnings
4. Privacy policy URL.
5. Terms URL: `https://dailycart.lk/terms-and-conditions`.
6. Refund URL: `https://dailycart.lk/refund-policy`.

## Upload Steps

1. Create app in Play Console.
2. Complete store listing.
3. Complete content rating questionnaire.
4. Complete data safety form.
5. Upload `app-release.aab`.
6. Create internal testing release.
7. Test with selected users.
8. Promote to production after approval.
