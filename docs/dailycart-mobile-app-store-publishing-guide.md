# DailyCart Apple App Store Publishing Guide

## App Information

App name: DailyCart  
Subtitle: Daily Essentials Delivered Fast  
Bundle ID: `com.dailycart.app`  
Keywords: `dailycart,grocery,delivery,shopping,sri lanka`  
Privacy policy: `https://dailycart.lk/privacy-policy`

## App Store Description

DailyCart makes daily shopping simple, fast, and reliable. Order groceries, fresh vegetables, fruits, household products, bakery items, pharmacy essentials, baby care products, personal care items, and daily necessities from your mobile phone.

Customers can search products, add items to wishlist or cart, apply coupons, use loyalty points, choose delivery address and schedule, pay through supported payment methods, track orders, receive notifications, submit reviews, and contact support.

DailyCart also supports vendor product and order management, rider delivery tracking, delivery proof upload, Firebase notifications, Google Maps, and secure Laravel Sanctum authentication.

## Required Assets

1. App icon.
2. iPhone screenshots.
3. iPad screenshots if iPad is supported.
4. Privacy policy URL.
5. Support URL.
6. Marketing URL if available.

## App Review Notes

Provide test credentials for:

1. Customer account.
2. Approved vendor account.
3. Approved rider account.

Explain that PayHere payment is used for Sri Lankan online payments and test cards should be used in sandbox mode if the app is not live.

## Submission Steps

1. Create app in App Store Connect.
2. Fill app information, pricing, privacy, and age rating.
3. Upload IPA from Xcode or Transporter.
4. Select build.
5. Add screenshots and descriptions.
6. Submit for review.
