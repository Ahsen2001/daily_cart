## Launch Summary

DailyCart Mobile App supports customer shopping, vendor product/order management, rider delivery handling, PayHere payments, Firebase notifications, Google Maps, Laravel Sanctum authentication, LKR pricing, and English-only user-facing text.

## Final Demonstration Flow

1. Customer registers.
2. Customer orders product.
3. Vendor confirms order.
4. Rider delivers order.
5. Customer receives order.
6. Customer reviews product.
